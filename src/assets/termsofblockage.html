Terms of Blockage

Master Trackers is most embedded in keeping its user-base safe against threats. Considering most of our user-base is children and family, it is essential to block attacks and follow country regulations. To keep our user-base safe, we have different types of bans/blockages that we can apply to the user. These bans include site-given blocks and Coudflare blocks

Types of Website Blocks:

  • Rate Limits - If you send too many requests to the website you will automatically be blocked for any time between 10 minutes and 1 year.
  • Country Regulations - considering this website is for kids, we have been requested by specific countries to ban access to our website.
  • IP Ban - Your IP has been blocked for anything from suspicious activity to innapropriate chats.

Types of Coudflare Bans:

  • Rate Limits - Incase I didn't catch it, cloudflare will automatically block you for overusing the website.
  • Known Attacks - Coudflare bans IP addresses known for attacking, hacking, spamming, etc. towards certain websites. You will be automatically blocked for any of these.
  • Browser or User-Agent Issues - Coudflare will block an incoming request if you are using a browser/user-agent that has validity or reliability issues.
  • VPN/Proxy Usage - You may be blocked for using a VPN/Proxy as it is against the rules of many websites. It can also lead to anonymous attacks.
  • Country Blockage - Some countries will request me to block access, so I may block via cloudflare.
  • IP Reputation - Multiple other websites may report your IP of illegal attempts of access, therefor requiring a block.

You may be automatically unblocked in the future.
