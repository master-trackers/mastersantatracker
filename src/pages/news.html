Shutdown of Big Tracker
Published on April 6, 2024 at 12:00 PM

Recently, as you may know, tebcc (track.easterbunny.cc) shut down, which was very unfortunate. Due to costs, his system was very hard to run, therefor, brings the shutdown of his software. It was very unfortunate as he was my only competition and now he's gone. It's the worst feeling you can feel. To honor his system, in 2025 we will be using his bunny image instead of our usual to honor him. It was such an honor watching tebcc grow, and now I'm inspired to create my own. This year was the second ever launch of mbt (master bunny trackers), and this year was the first public launch. It's sad to see tebcc go, but now we will rise. This program is growing fast, and has tons of moving parts. With version 0.5.0, we had implemented a lot of new features. Here is the 2024 systems information:
